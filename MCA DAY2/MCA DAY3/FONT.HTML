Social media: Online platforms that allow people to create,share and interact with content and with each other.

Foundation for AI generated content: Social media plays a significant role in communication and connection,allowing people to stay in touch with friends and family,regardless of location.

Diverse platforms: Social media platforms vary in their focus and features including social networking(example:Facebook,LinkedIn),media sharing(youtube,Instagram) and community based platforms. Marketing and business: Social media has become an essential tool for businesses to reach their target audience,promote their products and services and engage with customers.
