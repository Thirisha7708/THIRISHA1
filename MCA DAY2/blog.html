Iot device management

Internet of things (IoT) describes devices with sensors, processing ability, software and other technologies that connect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the Internet or other communication networks.[1][2][3][4][5] The IoT encompasses electronics, communication, and computer science engineering.

Connectivity: IoT devices are connected to the internet or other networks, enabling them to communicate and share data.

Interconnection: IoT allows for the interconnection of devices, systems, and services, often going beyond traditional machine-to-machine communication.
